ABOUT US

The Western Engineering Musical is a club that oversees the production of the annual engineering musical held at Western University. The club itself consists of the executive members, the production team, the backstage crew, the band, and the cast. The plots of the musicals are inspired from popular stories and are performed in a comedic fashion, as a parody to the original piece with Western Engineering themes. One very important part of the musical is the rehearsals which will occur throughout the school year and are attended by the cast, the band, and some of the production team and executive members. Please follow our social media accounts to stay up to date with the news and applications of the musical on facebook or Instagram!
